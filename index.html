Fresh From your own backyard.
This is an initiative to grow and connect locally sourced, naturally grown, healthy food without the use of synthetic fertilisers & harmful pesticides to our conscious consumers.

We encourage everyone to grow their own food for which handy growing guides are provided. We also want the growers to benefit by getting a better price for any excess produce they have by connecting them to the neighbourhood buyers. The consumer is benefited by getting better quality food than from an unknown source.
