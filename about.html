About Us Something about us

Grow My Greens Natural and Organic Movement

Grow my Greens is an initiative of Winning Earth where people are encouraged to invest in their health and their environment by farming and gardening organically.
We all prefer better tasting, more nutritious food that has a lower carbon footprint. Unfortunately in the times we live, we cannot guarantee the same about the food that is commercially available. This is where significance of this healthy, home-grown, local food movement.

We are dedicated to preserving the environment by providing you with cost-effective, state-of-the-art, organic growing supplies and the information and tools needed to apply them.
